About

We are Open for Everyone

Udyam's was opened with the initiative of teaching interested candidates everything that they needed to learn how to play carrom. We take players of all levels, from beginners to advanced. Special training is given to those who need it. We teach especially for playing in tournaments and such.

What we teach for

We teach mainly for playing in tournaments. Many of our students have won various championships in various districts. Care is given for each and every individual. We also provide the necessary tournament environment for the overall development of our students.

What We Teach

Technical shots, rotation of coins and taking good judgement under pressure.
